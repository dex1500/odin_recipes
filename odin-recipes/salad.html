Salad for dummies

This is salad everone can make! EVEN YOU, dummy! ;)

However, not only it is easy, but also it is delicious! Truly tasty and very good.

In order to eat it, you need to prepare it, which is very simple. You just need to prepare it and put it on a plate. So so easy!

And these are the things really need:

  • lettuce
  • little bit of lemon
  • olive oil
  • and also something else to eat, something that doesn't taste like air hahaha

As I CLEARLY mentioned earlier, this is a very easy thing to do and EVEN YOU should be able to do it. You only need to make sure that you follow these steps:

  1. Go to the shop
  2. Steal the lettuce, lemon and olive oil
  3. Haha only kidding you dummy - go back to the shop and pay to the nice lady
  4. Go back home
  5. Wash the lettuce
  6. Add some drops of lemon juice from the lemon you bought
  7. Add some drops of olive oil from the olive oil bottle you bought

And voila! Now you eat! (best with something else, like meat or sausage
