Magic Burger

This is the world's famous Magic Burger!

However, not only it is magic, but also it is magnificent! Truly remarkable and easy to make.

In order to eat it, you need to prepare it, which is very simple. You just need to prepare everything and put things between the two slices of a bun. So so easy!

And these are the things I'm talking about:

  • bun
  • beef burger
  • tomato
  • red oninon (it can't be a regular onion, it has to be red and if it's not then you die
  • yellow thing

As I CLEARLY mentioned earlier, this is a very easy thing to do and EVEN YOU should be able to do it. You only need to make sure that you follow these steps:

  1. Do the burger (medium is good)
  2. Separate two slices of the bun
  3. Put the burger on the bun
  4. Put the tomato on top of the burger
  5. Put the onion (RED!) on top of the tomato
  6. Put the yellow thing all over everything
  7. Put the second part of the bun on top of everything

And voila! Now you eat!
