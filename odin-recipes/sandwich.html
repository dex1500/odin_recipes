Posh cheese sandwich

This is a very good sandwich, one of the best there is!

However, not only it is good, but also it is magnificent! Truly fascinating and likeable.

In order to eat it, you need to prepare it, which can be difficult. But if you listen to the things I say, you will achieve success

And these are the things I'm talking about:

  • bread
  • cheese (best cheddar)

As I CLEARLY mentioned earlier, this is a very diffucult thing to do so you better follow these steps:

  1. Cut the bread (watch out not to cut yourself!)
  2. Cut the cheese (this is difficult - if you cannot do it, ask someone!)
  3. Put the cheese on bread

As I said, difficult but now ready! Now you eat!
